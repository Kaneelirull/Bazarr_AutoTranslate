# PROJECT PLAN - SRT Format Recovery and Dedicated Cue Repair

LAST UPDATED: 2026-07-21

## Goal

Recover conservative SRT formatting damage without AI, repair only remaining contaminated cues through a dedicated Lingarr line worker, and reliably synchronize repaired files with Bazarr.

## Success criteria

- Source-anchored blank-line and formatting damage is repaired without changing dialogue ownership.
- Ambiguous or contaminated cues alone are sent to Lingarr with one additional repair worker.
- Every AI repair attempt is safely logged and audited without subtitle text or credentials.
- Bazarr synchronization observes job startup and verifies repaired paths when item IDs are available.
- Regression, packaged Docker, startup, and dry-run checks pass.
- A new GitHub PR and `latest` plus dated Docker images are published.

## Decisions

- Recovery is source-anchored and conservative; ambiguous anchors are never guessed.
- `CLEANUP_REPAIR_WORKERS=1` is additional to `PARALLEL_TRANSLATES`.
- The repair queue is bounded at 100 tasks and overflow is deferred, not quarantined.
- Format-only fixes do not call AI.
- Successful repair stays at the original media path; quarantine remains the failure fallback.
- Repair logs contain metadata and validation outcomes, never full subtitle or context text.
- Quarantine, audit history, and logs retain the existing 30-day limit.
- Release branch: `codex/srt-format-recovery-repair-worker` from latest `main`.
- Docker release: `kaneelir0ll/bazarr-autotranslate:latest` plus the execution-date rollback tag.
